The Centro Estetico Luce is a large and bright Beauty and Wellness Salon, located in a green and quiet area of the city of Ravenna, convenient as a location thanks to the large parking lot. The Center is also served by new generation aesthetic machinery that is safe, performing and painless.

Our most requested treatments are: body exfoliants, real beauty rituals to relax and at the same time obtain a smooth and soft skin, without impurities; draining, anti-cellulite, lipolytic and toning body treatments with micronized medicinal herbs or algae added with pure essential oils and precious oils; facial treatments for a bright, compact skin and a homogeneous complexion, thanks also to the technologies of the machinery used: Oxys, a hyperbaric oxygen machine with which a vial based on hyaluronic acid and vitamins is infused, and Radio4, quadripolar radiofrequency to lift small wrinkles and redefine the contour of the face.
